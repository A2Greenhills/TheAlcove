America Runs on Dunkin

American work culture is characterized by what it lacks-- namely, breaks. In contrast to other countries, American employees send emails after hours, engage in workplace habits such as eating at their desk, and hardly ever go on vacation or take family leave.

While Americans pride themselves on their strong work ethic, other countries reportedly shun this style.
